How it works

A simple process that keeps you in control and gets you something you're proud to share.

1) Quick intake

You fill out the request form with the pages you would like to include (about, projects, experience, contact, etc.) along with style preferences and any content you have ready.

2) Content + layout

We organize your resume/projects into a strong narrative, then I draft a layout that matches your request (clean/modern, bold, dark, minimal, etc.).

3) Build + revisions

I build the page(s), and then do a revision so the final result feels sharp.

4) Launch

You get the site deployed (GitHub Pages FREE / custom domain if you have one), plus a simple guide to update content later.
